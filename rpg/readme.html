RPG Readme

This a single-character RPG made in Pico8. Currently only the first of four regions is playable. Your goal is defeat the first boss, the Dark Elf. Buy items at the shop. Rest at the inn. Learn spells from NPCs you find around the world. There is no XP, but you can get stronger by buying items and learning spells.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys - Move
  • Z - Confirm / Interact
  • X - Cancel

Items

  • Potion - Restores 30 HP
  • Bomb - Does 20 fire damage (ignores armor).

Spells

  • Sleep - Put an enemy to sleep. Damage wakes them up. You can escape from battle while the enemy is asleep.
  • Dispel - Removes all status effects from the enemy. This includes sleep, invisibility, and even armor.

Status Effects

Status effects show how many turns they last. You'll notice the pips ticking down every round.

  • Sleep - Cannot act. Cancelled by damage.
  • Invisible - Cannot take damage.
  • Armor - Reduces damage from attacks by half.
